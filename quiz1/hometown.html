About Jakarta

Jakarta is a vibrant metropolis that blends tradition and modernity. Jakarta has a mixture of traditional market and skyscrapers. You can say that Jakarta is a city that never sleeps with it's bustling streets and fast paced life

Historical Value

Jakarta holds significant historical value as the heart of Indonesia's colonial past. One of the most well known historical landmark is Kota tua. Although Jakarta is a bustling metropolis, Kota Tua would always be a reminder of what jakarta used to be

Modern Jakarta

Nowadays Jakarta is a bustling modern city that epitomizes Indonesia's rapid urban development. Modern Jakarta is known for its vibrant nightlife and cosmopolitan lifestyle. Even though Jakarta is a busy city, i feel like Jakarta has it's own charm by being that way.
